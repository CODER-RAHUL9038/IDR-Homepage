About IDR

Where academic insight meets industry practice.

Digital Risk Cyber Risk AI Governance NIST ISO 27001 NIS2

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute built for the realities of digital, cyber and technology risk in regulated, high-consequence environments. We exist to close the gap between the classroom and the frontline — producing practitioners who are technically confident, commercially aware, and ready from day one.

Through a formal partnership with a leading UK university, IDR blends peer-reviewed academic frameworks with live project experience. Our programmes are shaped by practitioners, for practitioners — giving students and professionals exposure to the tools, governance models and strategic challenges they will actually face across financial services, critical infrastructure and emerging technology sectors.

Whether you are starting out, seeking specialist upskilling, or looking to build out a risk function, IDR provides the structured pathway — from foundational training and bootcamps through to incubation, advisory and talent deployment.

Who We Serve

A community built for the risk-ready.

IDR is built around a growing community of students, early-career professionals and seasoned practitioners who share one goal: becoming genuinely effective in digital and cyber risk.

We serve individuals and organisations operating in regulated, high-consequence sectors — where the cost of poor risk practice is measured not just in financial penalties, but in operational disruption and public trust.
